The Problem

Service Providers Face Critical Payment Challenges:

  • Customers who delay or refuse payment after work is completed
  • Limited resources to pursue legal action for unpaid invoices
  • Severe cash flow problems from payment delays
  • Time wasted on collections instead of earning revenue
  • No way to properly vet customers before accepting jobs

Result: Financial instability and stress for hardworking professionals providing essential services

Our Solution

Comprehensive Payment Protection Membership

Core Services:

  • Payment guarantees
  • Customer vetting & credit checks
  • Automated payment processing
  • Legal support & debt collection

Additional Features:

  • Dispute mediation
  • Escrow services
  • Payment reminders
  • Contract templates

Value Proposition: Focus on your work while we ensure you get paid

Target Market

Who We Serve:

  • Independent plumbers, electricians, HVAC technicians
  • General contractors and specialized trades
  • Small service businesses (1-10 employees)
  • Annual revenue: $50K - $500K

Market Opportunity:

Millions of independent service providers and small contracting businesses face payment issues daily. This market is significantly underserved by existing financial services.
